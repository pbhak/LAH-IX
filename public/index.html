Flickpoint

Using body movements to control a computer

What is Flickpoint?

Flickpoint is a program that uses hand, head, or arm movement to control a computer. It also has speech to text for typing. This can very helpful for disabled people who can't use their hands to be able to control their device

How do you use Flickpoint?

After pressing the "launch" button below, there are many gestures that control the computer:

  • Move index finger around: Move cursor around
  • Move thumb, index, & middle finger together up/down: Scroll up/down
  • Move head up/down: scroll up/down
  • Move arm around: Move cursor around
